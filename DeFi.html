A crypto wallet is used to interact with the blockchain to send and receive crypto-
assets, and is a combination of public and
Private keys.
Essentially, each individual cryptocurrency
wallet address each have a unique Private
and public key. The public key is the wallet
address that is used to receive the correct
cryptocurrency on the same blockchain. The
private key allows access to the funds that
are related to the public crypto wallet address.

Cryptoassets

A Cryptocurrency is a type of currency which
uses digital files as money, Cryptocurrency
is a digital or virtual currency that is secured
by cryptography, which makes it nearly
impossible to counterfeit or double-spend.
Digital signatures can be used to keep the
transactions safe and let other people check
the transactions are real.
Bitcoin is the oldest and most popular
cryptocurrency in the world


Tether (USDT) (USD Tethered)


Tether is a stable coin, a type of
cryptocurrency which aims to keep
cryptocurrency valuations stable. USDT is
called a stable coin because of it originally
designed to always be worth USD $1.00.
Stable coins are digital currencies that are
tied to real-world assets like fiat currencies
or a stock or share.
